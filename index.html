Purpose of the Use of Force Form

The purpose of the Use of Force form is to document the facts, circumstances, actions taken, and outcomes of any incident in which force is used. It is critical for officers to complete this form because it creates a clear and consistent official record, supports supervisory and command-level review, reinforces accountability by requiring justification and documentation of actions, and helps demonstrate compliance with departmental policy and legal standards. Accurate completion of this form also supports internal investigations, training review, risk management, and defensible recordkeeping when incidents are later reviewed or challenged.
