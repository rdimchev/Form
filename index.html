Purpose of the Use of Force Form

This form is used to document incidents in which force was used or considered by an officer. The information collected helps ensure accurate reporting, accountability, and proper supervisory review in accordance with departmental policies. Officers should complete the form as soon as possible after an incident, providing clear and factual details about the circumstances, actions taken, and individuals involved. The report will be reviewed by supervisory staff as part of the department’s standard use-of-force documentation and oversight process.
